***| Data description***                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
| **ETS_Database_April_2026.xlsx (category 2)Verified emissions** | Verified emissions are the actual greenhouse gas emissions reported by each installation or operator for a given year, expressed in tonnes of CO2 equivalent. Please note that in some cases emissions may be reported with a delay, so data for the most recent year may still be incomplete.                                                                                                                                                                                                       
|**ETS_Database_April_2026.xlsx (category 4)Surrendered units** | Surrendered units are the allowances that operators hand back to the regulator to cover their verified emissions. In most cases, the two figures are equal, as operators are legally required to surrender exactly as many allowances as they emitted. Differences can occur due to late reporting or ongoing legal proceedings. Allowances used for surrender can come from different sources: free allocation, auctions, or the secondary market.                                                                

| **ETS_Database_April_2026.xlsx (category 1.3) Allowances auctioned or sold**| This category represents the number of allowances that each Member State sold through the official EU ETS auction platforms. Note that 1.3 reflects the number of allowances auctioned, not the revenues generated. It also includes allowances auctioned at EU level on behalf of specific funds such as the NER 300, the Innovation Fund, the Modernisation Fund and the Recovery and Resilience Facility, which appear as separate entries in the data.
